ABOUT AWARDS

TBZ has attended many award shows; ranging from MAMA, AAA, MMA, and Golden Disc Awards. The following is some of their biggest accomplishments.

Best Rookie Award

Their first award to date: the Best Rookie Award from the Sori-Bada music awards. This award was hosted by the Soribada Best K Music Awards, during 2018. The album that TBZ had been promoting was "Right Here".

Best New Male Artist Award

On one of the biggest Korean music award shows existing, The Boyz had won "Best New Male Artist Award" on the Melon Music Awards (or MMA). This is one of the best achievements to strive for during your early debut days, as its a highly competitive award.

Best Performance Award

Also a highly strived for award, the MMA Best Performance Award. There are hundreds of groups all giving performances of their lifetime and all attending the MMAs, so the competition is huge.

Best Performance Award

A second Best Performance Award, given at the Golden Disc Awards. The Golden Disc Awards (GDA) is a very high profile award ceremony. Even being invited there in the first place is a huge deal.

Artist of the Year

Last, but not least, at The Fact Music Awards, The Boyz won a Daesang for Artist of the Year. Daesangs are the highest tier award that can be given at any time to an artist, so being awarded this title is a huge deal.

Road To Kingdom

Road to Kingdom was Road to Kingdom was a television program that aired every week during 2020. RTK was a performance competition show in which 7 different groups competed to win the show.

There were 7 rounds in total, with only 1 week to prepare props, choreography, stage outfits, and songs before they had to be performed. The Boyz came in first place for nearly every round, and won the show in the end. Below are some of their best performances.

Danger

Danger (originally by Taemin) was the first full performance shown by TBZ on RTK. They performed the theme of robbers/thiefs stealing the crown. In this round, they also showcased many "magic tricks" and difficult stunts that blew many audience members away. This performance won first place.

REVEAL (Catching fire)

REVEAL (re-composed song by The Boyz) is the second part to Danger's story, where they lose the crown and have a revolution. This is done in fantastic story-telling using choreography and stage props. The Boyz use real fire and have huge flags they stand on held up by people during the climax. This performance was also ranked first.

Quasi Una Fantasia

The fourth round of the competition showed TBZ performing a re-vamped version of VIXX's Shangri-La. The Boyz explored a different style of dance for this stage: contemporary and ballet, to fit the theme of the song. It is a beautiful song paired with beautiful and intricate choreography. This performance came in second.
