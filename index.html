THE BOYZ

ABOUT THE BOYZ

The Boyz, also known as TBZ, are an 11 member K-pop group under the IST entertainment label. They debuted on December 6th, 2017 under a different label. Their fandom’s name is “Deobi” (pronounced DUH-BEE)

Lee Sangyeon

Born Nov. 4th, 1996, Sangyeon is the leader and main vocalist. He is the oldest in the group, and is often labeled as the “father”. His representative colour and number are red and 88.

Jacob Bae

Jacob was born May 30th, 1997. He is originally from Toronto, Canada. As the lead vocalist, Jacob has a sweet voice and a good hand for playing guitar. His representative colour and number are yellow and 30.

Kim Younghoon

Born Aug. 8th, 1997, Younghoon is a vocalist and 1/3 of the “visuals”. His english name is “Jelly”. Younghoon is also known as an actor in web dramas. His representative colour and number are sky blue, and 67.

Lee Hyunjae

Hyunjae was born on the 13th of Sep., 1997. He is lead vocalist, lead dancer, and also 1/3 of the “visuals”. He has a soft voice but is known for being blunt. His representative colour and number are silver, and 24.

Lee Juyeon

The most popular member, Juyeon was born Jan 15th, 1998. He is a main dancer, sub vocalist, and last 1/3 of the “visuals”. He describes himself as a quiet, understanding person. His representative colour and number are blue and 11.

Kevin Moon

Another Canadian, Kevin, who is a lead vocalist, was born Feb 23rd, 1998. He is a musical genius; Kevin has mastered many instruments and has written/composed many songs. He is also talented in the arts. His represent-ative colour and number are gold and 16.

Choi Chanhee

Chanhee, stage name New, was born April 26th, 1998. He is the main vocalist, and he attended a private music school to study singing. He describes himself as a sensitive person. His representative colour and number are transparency and 98.

Ji Changmin

Changmin, also known as Q, was born Nov 5th, 1998. He is well regarded as one of the best dancers in the industry; he is TBZ’s main dancer. He has a bubbly personality and loves horror. His representative colour and number are white and 02.

Ju Haknyeon

Born March 9th, 1999, Haknyeon is a vocalist and sub rapper. He once appeared on a huge debut show which garnered him a lot of fame pre-debut. He loves pigs because his family owns a pig farm. His representative colour and number are orange and 09.

Kim Sunwoo

Sunwoo, born April 12th, 2000, is one of the most talented rappers and producers of his generation. He has credits for lyrics on many of TBZ’s songs. He also placed very high on a rap show. His representative colour and number are purple and 19.

Eric Sohn

From L.A, Eric was born on Dec 22nd, 2000. He is the youngest member and acts as the lead rapper, and lead dancer. He is a hard worker and likes to give others energy. His representative colour and number are orange and 09.
